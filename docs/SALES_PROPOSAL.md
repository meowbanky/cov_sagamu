## Appendix C: Glossary of Terms

**Chart of Accounts:** List of all accounts used in accounting (Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Revenue, Expenses)

**Double-Entry Accounting:** Every transaction has equal debits and credits

**Journal Entry:** Record of a financial transaction (DR and CR)

**Trial Balance:** List of all accounts with their balances (must balance!)

**Period Closing:** End of month/year process to lock transactions and carry forward balances

**Bank Reconciliation:** Matching your book balance to the bank statement

**Audit Trail:** Complete history of all changes (who, when, what, why)

**IFRS/IAS:** International Financial Reporting Standards (accounting rules)
